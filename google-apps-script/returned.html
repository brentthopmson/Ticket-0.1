Your Ticket Has Been Returned
We wanted to inform you that your ticket transfer has been returned to the original sender/seller. This means the ticket is no longer in your possession and has been restored to the seller’s account.

This may have happened due to:
  • A disputed transfer that was not resolved within the 72-hour waiting period.
  • The sender/seller canceling the transfer before it was completed.
  • You or the recipient declining or failing to accept the ticket within the required timeframe.
If you still need this ticket, we recommend reaching out to the original sender/seller as soon as possible to discuss any potential next steps.
