Your Ticket Transfer is Disputed

The sender or seller of your ticket has disputed the transfer. As a result, the ticket will be held for 72 hours before being returned to the sender unless the dispute is resolved.

If you believe this was a mistake or wish to proceed with the transfer, we strongly recommend reaching out to the sender or seller directly to resolve the issue as soon as possible.

If the dispute is not resolved within 72 hours, the ticket will be automatically returned to the sender.

If you have any questions, please visit our help center or contact our support team.
